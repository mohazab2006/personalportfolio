# Vision

## Goal

Transform the portfolio into a premium, minimal, technically sharp portfolio designed for recruiters, hiring managers, and technical interviewers.

## What the site should communicate immediately

- Mohamed Azab is a serious builder
- He focuses on AI, cybersecurity, infrastructure, and real-world systems
- He has both project depth and real client / co-op credibility
- He can design and ship production-quality work

## Desired feeling

- premium
- calm
- modern
- sharp
- technical
- intentional

## Not the goal

- flashy student website
- maximalist cyber aesthetic
- animation-heavy experience
- long personal storytelling
- experimental interaction-first portfolio

## Success criteria

Within 5–8 seconds a recruiter should understand:

- who Mohamed is
- what kinds of roles fit him
- what his strongest projects are
- that he has real experience
- that the site feels polished and credible
