# Definition of Done

The portfolio is done when all of the following are true:

## Brand

- the site feels premium, minimal, and technically strong
- the site clearly targets AI, cybersecurity, infrastructure, and full-stack roles

## UX

- users land immediately on meaningful content
- the site is easy to scan
- the site is intuitive on desktop and mobile
- there is no unnecessary visual clutter

## Hero

- identity is clear within seconds
- positioning is strong
- CTAs are obvious

## Projects

- there are 4 featured projects
- KnowledgeMesh is visually dominant
- supporting cards are clean
- More Projects exists and feels organized

## Experience

- timeline is clean and premium
- CIRA and AmanahTech are represented well
- tiny experience images are gone
- entries are concise and high signal

## Stack

- no percentages remain
- stack is grouped clearly

## Snaggy

- Snaggy is integrated tastefully
- it enhances rather than dominates

## Mobile

- every section feels intentionally designed for mobile
- no section breaks down visually
- mobile interactions feel natural

## Code Quality

- reusable components
- maintainable structure
- consistent design tokens/patterns
- no leftover legacy clutter

## Final feeling

A recruiter should think:

“This candidate builds real systems and has excellent taste.”
