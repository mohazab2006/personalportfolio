# Brand

## Positioning

Mohamed Azab is a Computer Science student at Carleton University focused on AI/ML, cybersecurity, infrastructure, and engineering real-world systems.

## Brand traits

- technical
- disciplined
- modern
- ambitious
- practical
- systems-minded
- polished

## Tone

- concise
- confident
- clear
- not hype-heavy
- not overly casual
- not corporate and stiff

## Core message

Builds full-stack products, LLM-powered tools, automation systems, detection pipelines, and infrastructure-backed platforms.

## Visual identity goals

- premium dark interface
- restrained accent color
- mono + sans pairing
- crisp spacing
- strong grid
- subtle motion

## Avoid

- neon overload
- cyberpunk cliches
- gamer visuals
- too many accent colors
- cheap-looking glassmorphism
- visual noise
