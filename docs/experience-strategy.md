# Experience Strategy

## Goal

Keep the timeline concept, but redesign it to feel premium, clean, and recruiter-friendly.

## Experiences to show

- CIRA
- AmanahTech

## Timeline rules

- vertical timeline preferred
- strong spacing
- clear year/date markers
- each entry should feel like a clean content block
- avoid cramped presentation

## Content rules

Each role should include:

- role title
- company name
- date range
- 1–2 strong bullets
- optional tags

## Visual rules

- no tiny images
- no cluttered icons everywhere
- no messy lines or overdesigned nodes
- no excessive decorative treatment

## Messaging

AmanahTech should replace generic freelance naming.

Position it professionally.

## Outcome

The timeline should make experience feel credible, polished, and easy to scan in seconds.
