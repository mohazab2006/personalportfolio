# Design System

## Design goals

- premium
- restrained
- modern
- technically sharp
- high readability

## Colors

Use a dark base with one restrained accent.

No rainbow palette.

No multicolor visual overload.

## Typography

Use:

- clean sans-serif for headings/body
- mono for terminal cues, code-style labels, and subtle technical accents

## Spacing

Spacing should feel generous and premium.

Avoid cramped layouts.

Avoid excessive dead space.

## Borders and surfaces

- subtle borders
- soft separation
- minimal card treatment
- avoid loud shadows

## Motion

Motion should be:

- subtle
- fast
- purposeful
- secondary to content

No theatrical entry sequences.

No unnecessary animation loops.

## Cards

Cards should:

- feel product-grade
- have clear hierarchy
- prioritize screenshot/image size
- avoid overloaded tag stacks

## Images

Project visuals should be large and strong.

Experience thumbnails should be removed or replaced with better visual treatment.

Tiny visuals are not allowed.

## Responsive philosophy

Design for mobile intentionally.

Do not simply compress desktop layouts.
